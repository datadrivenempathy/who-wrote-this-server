Compliance and Removal Requests
This study leverages RSS feeds which are machine parseable documents that, among many uses, often inform search engines of new content availability. These data are collected in compliance with the Robots Exclusion Protocol by observing robots.txt files, industry-standard instructions which convey to systems like this crawler what content is permissible to process. Requests for removal of content can be emailed to the correspoding author: inquiry [at] datadrivenempathy.com. Requestors may need to conduct physical correspondence to verify identity.
